MCTT Youth Fellowship Church Calendar for 2021

MCTT Church is an organized denomination that runs almost the same program worldwide. They may live and worship in far-flung places, but MCTT Church worldwide are not only a faith community—they’re a family.

But finding a sense of togetherness can be difficult. One way MCTT Church can promote community across time zones is by celebrating special emphasis days and events together. The MCTT Youth Church calendar of Special Days and Events not only encourages unity, it also helps raise awareness of key issues, such as God Glorification, Child abuse, domestic violence, family and religious freedom.
